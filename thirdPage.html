Why the interviewer is asking this question:

The interviewer wants to know if you:
have done your research about their company; can describe the company well as an informed outsider; can translate what you know about the company into expressing interest.
The question is, in effect, a reverse sell. It may be the setup question for the interviewer selling you on the company, with the question acting as the baseline for what additional information they may need to tell you about. It is usually a good indicator that the interviewer is interested in you if they go into “sell” mode.
